Brokerage Comparison Worksheet

A printable worksheet for comparing brokerage costs, support, fees, training, and brand rules.

All downloads

Checklist

  • Monthly fees
  • Transaction fees
  • Commission split or flat fee
  • Broker accessibility
  • Training included
  • Advertising approval process
  • Technology and forms support
  • Exit or transfer terms

Notes

  • Run the worksheet under realistic transaction examples.
  • Do not choose solely by the headline split.
