Buyer Agent Transaction Checklist

A printable buyer-side transaction workflow from intake through closing.

All downloads

Checklist

  • Buyer intake complete
  • Financing or proof of funds reviewed
  • Search criteria entered
  • Offer terms conversation held
  • Earnest money deadline calendared
  • Inspection and due diligence dates tracked
  • Lender and closing office updates monitored
  • Walk-through and closing preparation complete

Notes

  • Customize all dates to the written contract.
  • Refer lending, legal, tax, and inspection questions to the appropriate professional.
