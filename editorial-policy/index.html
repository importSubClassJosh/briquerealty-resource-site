Editorial Policy

How Real Estate Education Content Is Published and Reviewed

This editorial policy explains who publishes the content, why it exists, how corrections are handled, and how AI-assisted drafting may be used.

Who Publishes the Content

Content is published by The Agent Resource Desk, the Editorial Team for Georgia Real Estate Agent Education. This is a role-based editorial desk identity for practical Georgia real estate education, not a fake individual human author.

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to publish practical real estate education, checklists, and plain-English guides for agents, investors, buyers, sellers, and property professionals.

Review Standard

Content is reviewed for clarity, usefulness, and compliance-minded presentation before publication. The site avoids fake statistics, fabricated quotes, guaranteed outcomes, and private brokerage procedure claims.

Corrections Process

Readers may request corrections by contacting contact@briquerealty.com or using the Contact page. Please include the page URL, the specific issue, and any public source that helps evaluate the concern.

AI-Assisted Content Statement

Content may be drafted, outlined, edited, or organized with AI tools. AI-assisted content should be reviewed before publication for accuracy, usefulness, clarity, and compliance-minded presentation.

No Legal, Tax, Financial, Lending, or Brokerage Advice

Content on this site is for general educational purposes only and is not legal, tax, financial, lending, or brokerage advice. Real estate rules, contracts, commissions, lending standards, and local practices may change. Readers should consult the appropriate licensed professional, broker, attorney, lender, tax professional, or local authority before making decisions.
